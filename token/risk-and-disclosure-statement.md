# Risk & Disclosure Statement

***



Paperless operates an AI-driven, tokenized trade finance infrastructure designed to connect stablecoin liquidity with real-world, insured, and over-collateralized commodity trade assets. While every measure is taken to minimize counterparty and operational risk, participants must understand and accept the following risk factors before engaging with the protocol.

***

### **1. Smart Contract & Protocol Risk**

* All Paperless contracts undergo **independent third-party audits** prior to mainnet deployment.
* Until audits are completed and published, users acknowledge that **smart contract vulnerabilities, exploits, or bugs** could result in loss of funds.
* The Paperless team employs **multisig-controlled deployments, testnet verification, and ongoing bug bounty programs** to reduce this risk.

***

### **2. Oracle & Data Reliability Risk**

* Paperless uses **multiple oracle sources and redundancy layers** to verify price, shipment, and credit data.
* Nonetheless, oracle latency, manipulation, or source failure could lead to **temporary valuation inaccuracies or transaction delays**.
* AI-based scoring is cross-checked against historical datasets and external verification nodes to ensure data integrity.

***

### **3. Insurance & Counterparty Risk**

* Every financed trade is insured through reputable P\&I clubs, warehouse insurers, and logistics underwriters, all listed in the verified Bill of Lading (B/L) pack.
* While all trades are insured, Paperless **cannot guarantee instant payout or insurer solvency**. Delays in claims or third-party disputes may occur.
* Counterparty verification is performed through AML/KYC and institutional-grade onboarding procedures.

***

### **4. Market & Liquidity Risk**

* Tokenized trade assets are backed by real invoices and collateral, but **secondary liquidity may be limited**.
* The Paperless team maintains an over-collateralization buffer of \~145% and a pool reserve of 25%, yet **extreme market conditions** could still impact liquidity or capital rotation.

***

### **5. Operational & Legal Risk**

* Paperless operates under evolving **digital asset and trade finance regulations**. Jurisdictional changes or policy updates could impact protocol operations.
* Participants are responsible for **ensuring compliance** with their local laws and tax obligations.

***

### **6. External Audits & Attestations**

* **Smart contract audits** are scheduled with independent security firms (to be announced pre-mainnet).
* **Financial and asset attestation audits** will be conducted through a registered third-party auditor upon institutional LP onboarding.
* Reports and attestations will be published in the **Paperless Transparency Portal** once completed.

***

### **7. General Disclaimer**

Participation in the Paperless ecosystem involves financial and technical risk. All users, LPs, and participants must conduct their own due diligence.\
Paperless does not provide investment, tax, or legal advice. Engagement with the protocol is entirely at the participant’s own risk.
