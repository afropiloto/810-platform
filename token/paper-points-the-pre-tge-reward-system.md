# Paper Points — The Pre-TGE Reward System

Think of this as like a hybrid between **Blast / EigenLayer’s pre-TGE points meta** and **DeFi loyalty programs**, but with **real yield mechanics** that actually reward _productive capital and combat impermanent loss with hedge strategies_, not just idle deposits.

***

## Paper Points — Rewards

Before the $PAPER token officially launches, early liquidity providers, partners, and vault users can **earn Paper Points** — the on-chain points system that determines early token allocations and bonuses.

Paper Points are designed to **bootstrap TVL, reward loyalty, and measure contribution** across the Paperless ecosystem.

***

### How It Works

* **1,000 Paper Points = 1 $PAPER Token** at TGE conversion.
* Points are earned by **contributing TVL**, **participating in liquidity programs**, and **referring new capital**.
* The system runs until **TGE**, when points automatically convert to $PAPER tokens via airdrop or claim contract.
* Points are non-transferable, but visible on-chain and in user dashboards.

***

### Points Architecture

* **TVL-weighted**: More liquidity = more points = higher $PAPER allocation.
* **Time-weighted**: Longer vault commitment = exponential point growth.
* **Networked**: Referrals and integrations grow total ecosystem TVL and reward contributors proportionally.

All activities are tracked through an **on-chain scoring contract** with real-time dashboards.

***

### Goal

> Scale vault TVL rapidly before TGE while building a transparent, gamified reputation layer.

Paper Points are not just airdrop bait — they measure the **true productivity of capital**.\
High-performing LPs and early vault participants will have a **priority seat in governance, yield multipliers, and staking tiers** once $PAPER goes live.

***

### Token Conversion Logic

At TGE:

```
Total Paper Points Pool → Fixed Allocation (e.g., 5% of $PAPER total supply)

User $PAPER Allocation = (User Points / Total Points) × 5% Supply
```

Example:

* Total ecosystem points: 100M
* User holds: 100,000 points
* TGE $PAPER allocation = 0.1% × 5% of total supply
