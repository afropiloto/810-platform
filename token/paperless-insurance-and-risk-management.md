# Paperless Insurance & Risk Management

Paperless integrates insurance and collateral layers to protect investors and LPs in the trade finance ecosystem. All cover and underwriting details are embedded in the verified, tokenized **Bill of Lading (B/L) pack**.

***

### 1. Types of Insurance & Coverage

* **P\&I Clubs (Protection & Indemnity):** Covers cargo, liability, and operational risks associated with shipping.
* **Warehouse Insurance:** Covers stored goods against loss, damage, and theft.
* **Underwriting Verification:** Every insurance policy referenced is included in the tokenized B/L pack, showing coverage terms and validity.

**Important Notes:**

* Claims history and issuer details are available in the pack.
* Insurance is explicitly verified before the AI Deal Desk approves funding.
* Coverage terms include whether principal, interest, and timely payment are protected.

***

### 2. Over-Collateralization & Circulating Pool Model

Paperless operates a **recirculating pool of invoices**, meaning financed invoices continuously generate value beyond the initial capital deployed.

* **Over-collateralization:** Average coverage is \~145% (principal + interest), ensuring there is always excess collateral value within the system.
* **Re-Circulation Effect:** As invoices mature and are repaid, funds are redeployed into new, insured invoices, compounding coverage and maintaining liquidity.
* **Pool Buffers:** Each pool holds an additional **25% capital buffer** to safeguard LP funds in the extremely rare event of default.
* **Default Rate Assumptions:** Historical default rates remain between **0.02%–0.1%**, far below the built-in buffer margin.

This layered structure ensures that the total value of invoices and collateral circulating in the system always exceeds the total financed capital, maintaining systemic solvency and protecting all LP positions.

***

### 3. Deal Desk Underwriting & De-Risking

* **Deal Desk Policy:** No deal is executed without verified insurance and proper collateralization.
* **De-Risking Measures:**
  * Only invoices meeting the insurance and collateral criteria are eligible.
  * AI risk models incorporate historical defaults, insurer reliability, and buffer overlays.
  * Real-time verification ensures all issued trade finance instruments are compliant.

**Outcome:** LPs gain confidence that their downside is protected through multiple, transparent layers — insurance, collateral, and AI-managed buffers.

***

### 4. Transparency & LP Reporting

* **Verified B/L Pack:** Shows insurer name, rating, policy details, coverage limits, and claims history.
* **Collateralization Data:** Explicit percentages, over-collateralization, and pool buffer values.
* **Default Assumptions:** Clearly quantified (0.02–0.1% on invoices), providing realistic downside protection.

LPs can therefore confidently assess the risk-reward profile of Paperless investments, knowing all coverage, collateral, and buffer layers are verified and immutable on-chain.
