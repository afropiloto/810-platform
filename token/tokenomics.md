### The Supply Shock Token

*

    How it Works for You:

    1. Revenue Engine: Every time a business pays back a loan, the protocol collects a fee.
    2. The Sweep: We don't hoard cash. The smart contract automatically uses that revenue to Buy $PAPER from the market.
    3. The Burn: Those bought tokens are instantly Destroyed.
       * _More Invoices Processed_ $$ $\rightarrow$ $$ _More Buy Pressure_ $$ $\rightarrow$ $$ _Less Supply._

    > The Upside: You are investing in a deflationary asset. As Paperless becomes the "Visa of Global Trade," the supply of $PAPER shrinks mathematically.
