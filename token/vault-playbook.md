# Vault Playbook

***

**Institutional Capital Framework**

### 1. Core Principles

Max Yield. No Liquidations.\
LPs own insured trade assets — not unsecured loans.

Always Overcollateralised\
70% max LTV ≈ 145% collateral coverage + buffer.

Predictable Liquidity\
Daily redemption gates + queue system.

Stablecoin Settlement\
All flows in USDC / USDT.

***

### 2. Vault Deployment Model

#### Capital Allocation

70–75%\
Short-tenor insured trade finance (30–180 days)

20–25%\
On-chain stablecoin buffer

***

#### Collateral Profile

• Legal title to goods\
• 100% insured principal\
• Diversified commodities\
• Diversified geographies\
• Diversified counterparties

***

### 3. Liquidity Mechanics

#### Standard Redemption

• 3% NAV daily gate\
• FIFO queue\
• Yield accrues until processed

***

#### Priority Redemption

Next-Day Exit → 0.75% fee\
Same-Day Exit → 1.5% fee

Fee Split:\
• 80% → LP pool\
• 20% → Treasury

Liquidity is engineered, not assumed.

***

### 4. Natural Liquidity Flow

Example: $100M Vault

$75M deployed\
$25M buffer

\~$3M/day redemption capacity\
\~$2.07M/day natural maturities

Peg stability without forced liquidation.

***

• No liquidation triggers\

This is structured credit, not yield farming.
