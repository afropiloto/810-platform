### Paperless – Stablecoin Yield Backed by Real World Trade

Crypto has liquidity.\
Global trade has yield.\
The bottleneck is processing.

Trade finance is a $trillions market still running on PDFs, emails, and manual underwriting. Deals take weeks to process. Capital can’t plug in at scale. That’s why stablecoins mostly sit in T-bills instead of productive real economy credit.

We fix the processing layer.

Paperless is an onchain finance and settlement engine for trade.\
Upload invoice + BOL → agents validate → compliant digital instruments are issued → stablecoin settlement executes.

Fully programmatic. No manual bottlenecks.

#### What This Means for Crypto

* Stablecoins fund real trade assets (invoices, shipping flows)
* Yield comes from short-duration trade finance spreads
* Settlement happens in stablecoins
* Assets are digitized and lifecycle-managed onchain
* Capital rotates as invoices settle

Instead of idle stablecoins earning base rate, capital flows into real-world yield with short tenor and structured risk controls.

#### The Model

1. Originator uploads trade docs
2. AI agents verify and structure
3. Trade asset is tokenized
4. Stablecoins fund the position
5. Settlement flows back on repayment
6. Yield distributed programmatically

No burn-and-mint chaos.\
No opaque offchain spreadsheets.\
The asset lifecycle is tracked and enforced digitally.

#### Why Now

Stablecoins (like USDC and USDT) have proven global settlement rails.\
Protocols like MakerDAO showed crypto-native demand for real-world yield.\
But scaling RWA yield requires automation, not more human credit desks.

We built the automation layer.

Paperless turns trade finance into a programmable yield engine for stablecoin capital.

Short duration.\
Asset-backed.\
Cash-flow driven.\
Onchain settlement.

Crypto liquidity meets real-world commerce — without TradFi bottlenecks.

Stablecoins shouldn’t just store value.\
They should finance the real economy and earn.
