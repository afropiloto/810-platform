# Key Components:



1. **Voice agent:** Automate finance applications reducing human delays and errors and streamlining scale and digital processes.
2. **AI Parsing Agent** - PDF and paaper document intake and structured data extraction from trade finance documents like letters of credit, invoices, and bills of lading.
3. **ERC-20 MLETR/ETDA** - Compliant digital twin documents(eBL, prom notes, invoice etc) fully verifiable and transferable, representing on chain legal ownership of the off chain assets.
4. **Deal Desk De-risking Agents** - Performs multi-dimensional risk assessment including credit risk, counterparty due diligence, sanctions screening, and country risk evaluation.
5. **ERC-8004 Invoice Financing Agent** - Evaluates invoice tokenization viability, manages smart contract deployment, and handles blockchain-based financing decisions.
6. **ERC-4626 Vaults:** Stores stable coin value and earns users shares, which automatically grow in value as the vault generates yield, and can be redeemed for their proportional share of the underlying assets and accumulated gains.

### Workflow Highlights:

* **Automated Processing**: Complete end-to-end automation from document receipt to financing decision
* **Risk-Based Decision Making**: Sophisticated scoring algorithms that consider multiple risk factors
* **Blockchain Integration**: ERC-8004 compliant invoice tokenization with smart contract automation
* **Compliance Built-in**: AML/KYC screening, sanctions checking, and regulatory compliance
* **Real-time Processing**: Target processing time under 5 minutes for standard applications

The system uses a decision matrix that weighs data quality, risk scores, and tokenization viability to automatically approve, reject, or flag applications for manual review. The ERC-8004 agent specifically manages the blockchain component, determining whether traditional financing or tokenized invoice financing is more appropriate based on ROI calculations and risk assessment.
