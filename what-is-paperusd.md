# What is paperUSD ?

#### The World's First AI-Verified, Invoice-Backed Stablecoin.

$paperUSD is a yield-bearing stable asset that puts your dollar to work in the real economy. Unlike traditional stablecoins that sit idle in bank accounts, $paperUSD is backed by a revolving pool of high-quality, short-term business invoices (Commercial Paper).

Your dollar acts like a bank lender: $1.00 = $1.00, but it accrues yield from global trade finance.

* Safety First: Every $1.00 of $paperUSD is backed by $1.43+ in real-world invoices and liquid cash reserves.
* Yield Source: Real interest paid by verified businesses, not inflationary token printing.

***

#### Architecture Flow: The Paperless Engine

1\. Deposit (Liquidity Injection) User deposits USDC or Fiat into the Protocol.

* _Status:_ User receives $paperUSD instantly.

2\. AI Origination Layer (The Gatekeeper) Instead of just "scanning markets," the Paperless AI Engine connects directly to business ERPs (Xero/QuickBooks). It analyzes thousands of data points—payment history, cash flow, and debtor risk—in milliseconds.

* _Action:_ The AI scores and approves only "Green Zone" invoices for funding.

3\. Securitization (The Mint) The protocol mints a Collateral NFT (pNFT) representing the legal claim to the approved invoice. This NFT is locked in the vault as the physical backing for the user's deposit.

* _Result:_ Liquid Cash goes to the business; Asset-Backed $paperUSD goes to the user.

4\. Yield Distribution As businesses pay back their loans with interest, the smart contract automatically routes the profit to $paperUSD holders.

* _User Experience:_ Your wallet balance grows automatically (Rebasing) or the token value increases (Accumulating), visible on your "AI Earnings" dashboard.

5\. De-Risk Engine (The Buffer) The system maintains a Cash Settlement Buffer (approx. 15% of TVL).

* _Function:_ If a user needs to exit, the protocol pays them from the cash buffer instantly, without needing to sell an invoice.

***

#### Key Components

AI Risk & Credit Engine Replaces the human bank manager. It reads invoices, verifies bank data, and assigns a dynamic Risk Score to every potential borrower before a single cent is lent.

The Revolving Liquidity Pool A single, unified capital pool that funds thousands of diverse invoices. This ensures your $paperUSD is diversified across many industries (Logistics, SaaS, Construction), reducing concentration risk.

Ephemeral Collateral Vault Holds the "Digital Title Deeds" (pNFTs) to every invoice. As invoices are repaid, the pNFT is burned, ensuring the ledger always reflects live debt only.

Stable Wrapper ($paperUSD) An ERC-20 token that represents your share of this high-yield trade economy. It is fully composable with DeFi (can be used as collateral or in LPs).
