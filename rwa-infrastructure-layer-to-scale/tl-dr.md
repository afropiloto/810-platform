#### What Is Paperless Labs?

Paperless Labs is an infrastructure layer that:

1. Scales tradfi and issues tokenised credit.
2. Deploys liquidity into Real World Assets (RWAs) like supply chain finance.

Instead of your money sitting idle in a wallet, it joins a Revolving Liquidity Pool. This pool lends capital to businesses buying inventory. When they sell the goods and pay back the loan with interest, that profit goes directly to you.

#### How It Works (The Flywheel)

1. Mint: You swap USDC for $paperUSD.
2. Verify: Our AI Risk Engine scans and approves a real-world invoice (collateral).
3. Fund: Your capital flows from the Vault to the borrower.

5. Yield: The interest flows automatically into your $paperUSD balance.

It’s like being the bank for a massive network of merchants. You provide the working capital they need to operate; they pay you a consistent "thank you" fee every time they complete a trade.

#### Core Components

1\. $paperUSD (The Product)

An AI-verified, yield-bearing stablecoin. It is not backed by empty promises, but by a diversified pool of short-term commercial debt.

2\. The Revolving Vaults

Smart Contract pools that hold the collateral. They automatically manage the flow of funds between depositors and borrowers, ensuring solvency.

3\. AI Risk Engine

Our proprietary AI agents connect to business ERPs (Xero/QuickBooks) to score risk, predict defaults, and reject bad invoices before they are funded.

4\. The Settlement Buffer

A layer of liquid cash (approx. 15% of TVL) always kept on hand to ensure instant withdrawals for users.

5\. Insurance & Safeguards

Loans are over-collateralized (targeting $1.43 backing for every $1.00 issued) and backed by credit insurance partners where applicable.

***

#### Vaults: The Engine Room

Think of the Vault as a Digital Clearing House.

* Input: Users deposit liquidity.
* Action: The Vault purchases verified "Commercial Paper" (Invoices).
* Security: Every loan is backed by legal title over real goods or receivables.
* Ratio: We maintain an over-collateralization ratio (e.g., holding $143 in assets for every $100 lent) to absorb any shocks.
* Reward: The profit from these loans is the source of your APY.

***

#### 💲 Tokenomics

* Backing: Elastic supply. Tokens are only minted when eligible collateral (Cash or Invoices) enters the vault.
* Yield Source: Purely from Tokenised Real World Revenue (Trade Finance credit), not inflation.
* Governance: The $PAPER pays for transactions fee management token manages the risk parameters (LTV, Sector Limits).

***

#### Risk & Mitigation

We replace "Trust" with "Verification."

* Risk: Borrower Default.
  * _Mitigation:_ Over-Collateralization ($1.43:$1.00) ensures the vault stays solvent even if a loan fails.
* Risk: Asset Devaluation.
  * _Mitigation:_ Short Duration. We fund invoices due in 30-60 days, minimizing exposure to long-term market shifts.
* Risk: Fraud.
  * _Mitigation:_ AI Surveillance. Our agents monitor borrower bank accounts in real-time to detect suspicious activity.

#### FAQ

Is this risk-free?

No investment is risk-free. However, our stable coins are designed to be Market-Neutral. It is not exposed to crypto price volatility, only to the credit risk of verified businesses, which is mitigated by our AI Engine and over-collateralization.

Why are the yields so high?

Because we scale the shetre volume txn process x1000, reduce human costs, manual process fees, port delays, insurance costs and other countery party costs as well as faster capital and settlement fees up to 24% APR still means an increased profits for fast liquidity. We simply pass that yield to you instead of keeping it like a bank.

Can I exit anytime?

Yes. The protocol maintains a Liquid Cash Buffer to facilitate instant redemptions but loos yields.

#### Glossary

* Yield: The real-world interest earned from financing trades.
* RWA (Real World Asset): The physical invoice or inventory backing the digital token.
* Vault: The smart contract pool that holds assets and manages loans.
* Collateralization: The ratio of Assets to Liabilities (e.g., $1.43 Assets backing $1.00 Token).
