# Paperless Onboarding & Scoring Agent

Paperless leverages a **fully automated KYC/KYI/KYB scoring agent** to streamline onboarding and finance pre-DD (due diligence) opportunities from corporates, banks, and non-bank trade finance institutions. This allows Paperless to finance high-quality deals that might otherwise go unfunded, even if the originating institution does not provide funding. All deals assume a **maximum historical default rate of 0.1%**, reflecting the conservative risk model. Both **Tier 1 and Tier 2 participants** can access Paperless financing with risk-adjusted scoring thresholds.

***

### 1. Overview

The Paperless Onboarding & Scoring Agent is designed to:

* **Verify identity and eligibility** of corporate and institutional participants.
* **Assess financial health, transaction quality, and creditworthiness** of deals before traditional DD.
* **Automate approvals** for Tier 1 and Tier 2 institutions and high-value trades.
* **Integrate seamlessly** with Paperless AI Deal Desk and tokenized finance vaults.

This system allows Paperless to scale trade finance without relying solely on manual processes, reducing friction and time-to-funding.

***

### 2. KYC/KYI/KYB Automation

**Key Components:**

* **KYC (Know Your Customer):** Corporate identity verification through government IDs, business licenses, and corporate registries.
* **KYI (Know Your Institution):** Verification of institutional status, banking licenses, trade finance credentials, and regulatory compliance.
* **KYB (Know Your Business/Beneficial Owner):** Ensures the legitimacy of the corporate entity, shareholders, and controlling parties.

**Automation Features:**

* AI-driven document verification and OCR extraction.
* Cross-referencing with global databases (GLEIF, national registries, trade associations).
* Continuous monitoring and alerting for sanctions, PEPs, or risk changes.

**Outcome:** Verified Tier 1 and Tier 2 corporates, banks, and non-bank institutions can onboard **without lengthy manual checks**, ready for pre-DD finance.

***

### 3. Deal Scoring Agent

**Purpose:**

* Automatically assesses submitted trade finance deals based on quality, creditworthiness, and risk.
* Enables Paperless to fund **good deals even if originating institutions do not provide financing**.
* **Default exposure is capped at a maximum of 0.1%** per invoice pool, reflecting historical performance and over-collateralization buffers.

**Scoring Criteria:**

1. **Invoice Quality:** Validated through tokenized Bill of Lading packs.
2. **Insurance Coverage:** P\&I, warehouse, or trade insurance verified on-chain.
3. **Collateralization & Buffers:** Over-collateralization, pool buffer percentages, and default risk overlays.
4. **Institutional Tier:** Tier 1 and Tier 2 corporates or banks are prioritized, with tier-adjusted thresholds to manage risk.
5. **Historical Performance:** Prior repayment history, defaults, and trade execution reliability.

**Scoring Mechanism:**

* AI assigns a composite score to each deal.
* Deals meeting threshold are automatically **approved for financing via Paperless vaults**.
* Lower-scoring deals may trigger **manual review or additional collateral requirements**.

***

### 4. Tiered Pre-DD Financing

**Key Insight:**

* Paperless targets **high-quality deals** that are typically overlooked by originating institutions.
* Verified Tier 1 and Tier 2 participants receive **fast-track financing**, enabling corporates to access liquidity instantly.
* Pre-DD financing ensures that **capital deployment is precise, insured, and over-collateralized**, protecting LPs and investors.
* **Maximum default rates remain 0.1%**, ensuring predictable, low-risk yield for LPs.

**Benefits:**

* Reduces funding gaps in global trade.
* Maximizes capital efficiency through automated scoring and onboarding.
* Strengthens Paperless ecosystem credibility by funding only **high-value, low-risk trade flows**.

***

### 5. Integration with Paperless Vaults & AI Deal Desk

* Once onboarded and scored, deals flow directly into **Paperless tokenized vaults**.
* AI Deal Desk monitors ongoing performance, insurance coverage, and collateral levels.
* Automated alerts trigger **risk-adjusted capital redeployment**, maintaining pool health and investor confidence.

**Outcome:** Paperless creates a **self-sustaining, high-quality trade finance network**, fully compliant, automated, and ready for institutional-level deployment.

***

### 6. Transparency & Reporting

* All verification and scoring data is recorded **on-chain and in secure ledgers**.
* LPs and auditors can access:
  * Institutional verification status (KYC/KYI/KYB).
  * Deal scoring rationale.
  * Insurance coverage and collateralization metrics.
* Provides **pre-DD visibility** while ensuring privacy and regulatory compliance.

***

**Summary:**\
The Paperless Onboarding & Scoring Agent enables **automated, risk-adjusted financing** for Tier 1 and Tier 2 corporates, banks, and non-bank trade institutions. By combining KYC/KYI/KYB, AI scoring, and tokenized collateral, Paperless ensures capital is deployed **efficiently, securely, and transparently** into the best global trade finance deals — with **maximum historical default rates of 0.1%**.
